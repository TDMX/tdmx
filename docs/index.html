TDMX

Trusted Domain Messaging eXchange

by Peter Klauser

a specification which enables secure B2B and M2M communications between separate corporations via the cloud.

a potential new standard

the presentation below describes the motivation for creating TDMX and why it makes sense to develop it further as a standard.

trust

TDMX solves the fundamental problem of trust in distributed communication ensuring end2end messaging only between trusted participants.

complete

TDMX is a complete system specification, defining concepts of identity, authentication, authorization, addressing, sending, receiving, flow control and manageability. The specification intends to enable interoperability between cloud service providers and a tool chain which can work with all service providers.

confidentiality

TDMX aims to provide best possible confidentiality of your data. Even a service provider operated by the NSA should not be able to decrypt your messages, now or in the forseeable future. Even if your ServiceProvider was in posession of your keys, or would be forced to provide your message data to some third party who was in posession of your keys, your data would still not be decryptable!
